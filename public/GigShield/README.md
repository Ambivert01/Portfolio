# Vision

Millions of India's gig workers lose income every year because of events beyond their control.

Heavy rain.

Floods.

Extreme heat.

Air pollution.

Traffic shutdowns.

Government restrictions.

Platform outages.

Current insurance products protect vehicles, health, or life.

They do **not** protect lost earnings.

GigShield changes that.

It introduces a fully automated AI-powered parametric insurance system where workers receive compensation automatically whenever verified external disruptions prevent them from earning.

No paperwork.

No claim forms.

No waiting.

Just protection.

---

# Problem Statement

Platform-based delivery workers operate on daily income.

Missing even one working day can reduce weekly earnings significantly.

Current insurance products fail because

- They insure physical damage.
- They require manual claims.
- They involve lengthy verification.
- They are expensive.
- They are difficult to understand.

GigShield insures

**Income Loss**

instead of

**Damage.**
